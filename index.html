CREATE A COMMUNITY

Designed to be a place where your community could assemble, discuss, share and vote on meaningful topics.


People love to share and discuss the things they are passionate about with like-minded people. In Crowdhound, You decide how potential participants sign up. Authenticate users with their Facebook or Twitter account, or based on their phone number, or using traditional username and password registration. Alternatively, Crowdhound can piggyback off your existing application’s user database and login scheme.


Other Features

  • Choose your look from our existing themes or use your own.
  • Organise topics by category or customise as required.
  • Share related video or website links to posts on a specific topic.
  • Be notified immediately when someone responds to one of your posts or mentions you in their own posts.
  • Create calendar events as private, public or tag specific users or groups.
  • Vote up or down a post. This allows posts with the highest number of cumulative votes to be shown at the top of a discussion thread.
